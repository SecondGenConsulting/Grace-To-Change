About Us

Grace To Change. Established 2015.

What we're about.

At Grace To Change we believe that change is possible for everyone. We provide the tools, support, and hope for a life free from addiction. If you, or someone you love, needs help please seek Grace To Change for help through our 2 programs below.

Grace To Change Drug Testing

At GraceToChange, we utilize a multi-modal approach to counseling, including psycho-educational groups, process groups, and an eclectic blend of individual counseling techniques designed to meet each client's specific need. Let us help tailor your recovery program just for you! You can find our more about the prgoram and how it can help by clicking learn more.

Tiny House, Big Recovery

Empowering women through providing a safe sober living environment while offering tools and support to enhance their future and achieve long term recovery.We have chosen the tiny house model because of its small price tag but individual living space. These homes will be placed in a community that will foster mutual support and accountability.
