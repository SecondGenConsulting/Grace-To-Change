Tiny House Big Recovery

Change a life, change your community!

Why do we need Tiny House Big Recovery?

Empowering women through providing a safe sober living environment while offering tools and support to enhance their future and achieve long term recovery. We have chosen the tiny house model because of its small price tag but individual living space. These homes will be placed in a community that will foster mutual support and accountability.

What will we offer?

If you have an assaultive offense, a robbery, or a child endangerment charge you are unable to utilize The Samaritan Inn. If people do not have healthy shelter they tend to go back to the same environment they were in when they were arrested.

What will we offer?

Each resident will receive Intensive and Supportive Outpatient substance abuse treatment.

Get Involved Today

We are actively searching to purchase land, receive a land donation or possible rent a home that has land attached to it. Once that is established we will be purchasing the tiny houses and working on getting those ready for the women. We are asking for assistance from roofers, plumbers, electricians, or any other tradesmen that would be willing to donate their time and or supplies to this mission. All donations will go towards these immediate needs.
